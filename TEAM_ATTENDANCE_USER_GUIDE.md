## ğŸ” Troubleshooting

### Q: I don't see the "Team Attendance" option
**A**: This feature is only available for Leads and Managers. Regular employees can only access "My Attendance".

### Q: Attendance list is empty
**A**: 
- Check if you're looking at the right date
- Ensure your team has checked in for the day
- Try pull-to-refresh
- Verify you're in the correct office

### Q: Statistics show zero
**A**: 
- Statistics show current month data
- If it's a new month, data will be limited
- Historical months can be added in future updates

### Q: Can't see all team members
**A**: 
- You only see employees from your assigned office
- Team members must check in to appear
- Absent members won't show in today's list
